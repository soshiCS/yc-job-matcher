YC Job Matcher

Step 1: Index jobs to build your database. Step 2: Match your résumé against it — cheaply.

1 · Index jobs

Scrape jobs from YC, profile each (role, seniority, skills), and save to the database. No résumé needed.

Automatically continues from what's already saved — skips jobs already in the database and grabs new ones (newest first).

2 · Match my résumé

Profile your résumé, shortlist the most similar saved jobs by skill overlap, then score just those.

Maintenance

Remove jobs from the database that are no longer live on YC (filled or removed).
